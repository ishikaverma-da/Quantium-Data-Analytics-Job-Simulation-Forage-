# Quantium Data Analytics Job Simulation (Forage)

## 🛒Retail Analytics Case Study | Python | Customer Segmentation | Business Insights


This repository contains my solution for the **Quantium Data Analytics Job Simulation on Forage**. The project focuses on analyzing retail transaction and customer purchase behaviour data to generate actionable business insights and commercial recommendations for the Category Manager.

---

# 🎯 Project Objective

The objective of this project was to analyze customer purchasing behaviour and identify key business insights that support strategic decision-making in the chips category.

The project covers:

- Data Cleaning
- Feature Engineering
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Customer Segmentation
- Data Visualization
- Business Insights
- Strategic Recommendations

---

# 📂 Dataset

This analysis uses two datasets provided as part of the Quantium Job Simulation.

- QVI Transaction Data
- QVI Purchase Behaviour Data

The datasets contain customer transaction records, product information, customer lifestage, and premium customer segmentation.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- 🥇 Kettle was the highest-performing brand in terms of quantity sold.
- 👥 Older Singles/Couples generated the highest total sales.
- 💎 Mainstream customers contributed the largest share of revenue.
- 📦 175g was the most preferred pack size.
- 📊 Customer purchasing behaviour varied across lifestage and premium segments, creating opportunities for targeted marketing and inventory planning.

---

# 📌 Business Recommendations

- Focus promotional campaigns on high-performing brands such as **Kettle**.
- Prioritize **Older Singles/Couples** and **Mainstream** customers.
- Maintain sufficient inventory of **175g** pack sizes.
- Use customer segmentation to design targeted marketing campaigns.
- Apply purchasing behaviour insights to improve category planning and commercial decision-making.
